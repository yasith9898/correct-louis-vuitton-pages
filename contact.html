Information regarding delivery, exchanges, and returns of online orders.

Delivery Options

Please note that adding a personalization service will extend your delivery by several weeks depending on the product category.

Saturday Delivery

Saturday delivery service available for orders placed Sunday - Friday.

Next Business Day Delivery

1 business day service available for orders placed Monday - Friday.

Standard Delivery

This delivery option has less carbon emissions than Air Delivery (on average for US mainland shipments). Receive your order within 1-5 business days.

Collection-Store

Have your order delivered to the store of your choice within 1 business day (available in select Louis Vuitton stores).

Exchanges (must be within 30 days of purchase)

Online Exchange

Initiate your exchange in your MyLV account

In-Store Exchange

Bring your purchase to a Louis Vuitton store to exchange in person (except Bloomingdale's, Macy's, and Nordstrom)

Exchange with an Advisor

Connect our Client Services team to initiate your exchange

Returns (must be within 30 days of purchase)

Online Returns

Initiate your return in your MyLV account

In-Store Return

Bring your purchase to a Louis Vuitton store to return in person (except Bloomingdale's, Macy's, and Nordstrom)

Return with an Advisor

Connect our Client Services team to initiate your return.
